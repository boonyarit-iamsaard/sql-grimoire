# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Working developers — comfortable writing everyday SQL, aiming for real database
mastery (schema design, constraints, transactions, concurrency, indexing, query
plans). They arrive solo, in a browser, in self-directed learning time; the job
is to solve a realistic production incident and come away understanding _why_
the solution works. The current audience is playtesters answering one question:
does finishing mission one make you want mission two?

## Product Purpose

SQL Grimoire is a browser-based SQL-learning RPG: an embedded database,
result-graded challenges, and a narrative world where missions are production
incidents framed as quests. Today it is a validated one-mission prototype; the
long-term goal is a commercially viable database-mastery platform for
individual developers, teams, and bootcamps. Success for the prototype is a
playtester finishing "The Missing Shipment" and asking for more.

## Brand Personality

Cozy arcane scholar. A warm candlelit study: the grimoire, a patient mentor,
quiet magic. Learning as ritual — calm curiosity and earned confidence, never
pressure, never noise. Three words: warm, scholarly, quietly-magical.

## Anti-references

- **Corporate LMS** — Coursera/Udemy course-card grids, progress percentages,
  certificate energy.
- **Candy gamification** — Duolingo-style streaks, confetti, mascot nagging;
  gamification as sugar coating.
- **Generic SaaS dashboard** — sidebar + stat cards + charts; the default dark
  admin template.
- **Grimdark fantasy** — skulls, blood reds, oppressive darkness; fantasy taken
  too seriously.

## Design Principles

1. **The error message is the mentor.** SQLite's output is the pedagogy — never
   hide, soften, or gate it behind client-side validation.
2. **Workbench first, story as candlelight.** Narrative frames the work and
   then gets out of the way; the editor, results, and schema are the primary
   surface on every mission screen.
3. **Earned, quiet rewards.** Completing a mission feels like mastering a
   spell — weighty and satisfying, not slot-machine confetti. Reward gravity
   matches effort.
4. **Hand-crafted, not asset-packed.** All art is authored SVG, all sound is
   synthesized; every decorative element should feel drawn for this world.
5. **Calm density.** Developer-tool precision: crisp monospace surfaces,
   readable result grids, no dashboard chrome.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline: ≥4.5:1 body-text contrast, full keyboard operability across
the workbench, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for every animation, and
no information conveyed by sound alone.
